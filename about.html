About This Dashboard

This dashboard is meant to be an exploratory tool to look at the spread of COVID-19 in the state of California. It specifically is it assess each county and how it is doing in relation to the guidelines set out by the California Department of Public Health https://covid19.ca.gov/roadmap-counties. Any descisions made about reopening each individual county are still up to the discretion of each countie's local government, regardless of where they are on this roadmap.

Authored, created, and maintained by Eric Junkins. Any bug notifications, suggestions for improvements, or comments can be sent to eric.junkins@jpl.nasa.gov

Resources

  • COVID-19 Cases/Deaths Data: COVID Data is pulled from two different sources, and then cross checked against each other to verify accuracy.
    • https://covidtracking.com
    • https://github.com/nytimes/covid-19-data
    California's current data quality grade is "B" as assigned by the Covid Tracking Project. To learn more about how this data grade is assigned you may visit https://covidtracking.com/state-grades
  • COVID Testing data for the state of California is supplied by https://covidtracking.com. More granular testing data at the county level is eventually desired instead however.
  • California population pulled from https://www.california-demographics.com/counties_by_population
